Harry Potter

Harry Potter is the central character of the Harry Potter series, written by J.K. Rowling. He is known as "The Boy Who Lived" after surviving the killing curse from Lord Voldemort, the most feared dark wizard of all time. Orphaned as a baby, Harry was raised by his neglectful aunt and uncle, unaware of his magical heritage until his 11th birthday, when he received a letter inviting him to attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.
