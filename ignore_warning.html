Ignoring the Warning

Despite Professor McGonagall’s warning, you decide to continue your search. Late at night, you sneak into the dungeons and uncover a hidden passage leading to an ancient chamber.

Inside, you find a mysterious pedestal with a glowing artifact. As you step closer, the air crackles with magic.

Suddenly, you hear footsteps—someone else is here.

Congratulations! You have discovered the Forbidden Chamber, but who knows what secrets or dangers lie ahead?

The End.
