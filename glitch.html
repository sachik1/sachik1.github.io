Glitch Art

A glitch is an intentional, sometimes accidental, distortion of digital images to display a message. These images represent the present and future of America. The American Flag and Great Seal are glitched to reflect division in the country. A shadow over the dollar bill foreshadows that we, as a nation, may not be heading in the right direction.
