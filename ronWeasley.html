Ron Weasley

Ron Weasley is Harry Potter's best friend and a member of the Weasley family. He is known for his loyalty, humor, and courage. As part of the trio with Harry and Hermione, Ron plays a crucial role in their adventures, providing unwavering support despite his fears. He comes from a large, loving family and is a key member of the Gryffindor house at Hogwarts.
