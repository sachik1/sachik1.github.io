The Ancient Vault

You whisper **"Alohomora,"** and the door creaks open, revealing an **ancient underground vault**.

Inside, a golden artifact rests on a pedestal, radiating powerful energy.

Suddenly, the air shifts. A **guardian spirit appears**, demanding to know why you've entered.

You must make a final choice:

  • Try to reason with the guardian.
  • Grab the artifact and run.
