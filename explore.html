Sneaking into the Restricted Section

Late at night, you and your friend sneak into the restricted section of the library. By the light of your wands, you search through ancient scrolls and forbidden books.

Finally, you find an old parchment detailing a **hidden chamber** beneath Hogwarts. But before you can read further, you hear footsteps approaching.

Panicked, you grab the parchment and run. The next day, you decipher the clues and follow the hidden path...

You have risked everything and uncovered the secret location!

The End.
