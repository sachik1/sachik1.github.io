Library Research

You and your friend head to the Hogwarts library, searching through ancient texts and forgotten tomes. After hours of research, you find a reference to a hidden chamber beneath the castle.

It seems only a powerful spell can reveal the entrance. You practice the incantation and, later that night, head to the location described.

As you speak the words, the ground trembles and a hidden staircase appears...

Congratulations! You have uncovered the entrance to the hidden chamber.

The End.
